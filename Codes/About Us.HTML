Business History




Mariam’s Baked Pastries, founded by Mariam D. El-Ali in 2019, is an online cookie shop which she established using the Instagram platform. They offer various cookies on their menu. The customer has an option to either pick up their orders from the shop or to request delivery service. The business relies on delivery applications such as Grab and Lalamove to deliver the orders to their customers.  The shop bakes their orders fresh daily. They deliver orders within the same day which limits the number of orders they can accept in a day.  They recently expanded their platform through opening their online shop using the following platforms such as Lazada and Shopee.
